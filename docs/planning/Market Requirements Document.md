### Market Requirements Document (MRD): StackAlchemist

**1. Executive Summary**
The global AI code generator software market is shifting from localized autocomplete plugins toward systems capable of scaffolding entire applications. StackAlchemist enters this market by bridging the gap between static SaaS boilerplates and open ended, autonomous coding agents. It provides the reliability of a manually coded stack customized instantly to the user's exact schema.

**2. Target Demographics**
* **Primary Segment: Freelance Developers & Solo Engineers.** Individuals running multiple micro SaaS projects who resent unpaid hours spent on boilerplate configuration.
* **Secondary Segment: Non Technical Founders.** Entrepreneurs with business logic who lack technical skills or capital to hire an agency for baseline infrastructure.
* **Tertiary Segment: Technical Agency Owners.** Development shops looking to accelerate the initial delivery phase of client contracts.

**3. Customer Pain Points**
* **Boilerplate Fatigue:** Exhaustion from repeatedly configuring OAuth, CRUD endpoints, and ORMs.
* **The "Blank Canvas" AI Problem:** General LLMs hallucinate file structures and lose context halfway through generating a complex application.
* **Static Boilerplate Rigidity:** Purchasing a static boilerplate still requires days of manual refactoring to fit specific database schemas.
* **Deployment Friction:** Provisioning AWS infrastructure is a massive barrier to entry for non technical users.

**4. Competitor Analysis**
* **IDE Assistants (e.g., GitHub Copilot):** Excellent for inline autocomplete but cannot autonomously generate a multi tiered architecture from a single prompt.
* **Browser Based Agents (e.g., Bolt.new, v0):** Exceptional at rapid UI prototyping but output unopinionated backend code lacking enterprise grade rigor.
* **Static SaaS Boilerplates (e.g., ShipFast):** Provide a proven repository but are entirely static and require manual integration of custom data models.

**5. Product Positioning and Unique Value Proposition (UVP)**
StackAlchemist positions itself as an AI driven architect outputting strict, enterprise grade infrastructure. 

**UVP:** StackAlchemist combines the structural guarantee of a premium static boilerplate with the dynamic generation of an AI agent. It utilizes a Compile Guarantee CI/CD pipeline, ensuring users receive a working .NET/Next.js stack customized to their exact requirements without the risk of AI hallucinations.
